Hello! I'm Jessica, a L.A.-based product designer who’s all about design that’s transformative, human-centered and makes the world a better place.

​

A sample of what I've done


​
​

Turning a paper-heavy, error-prone payroll process fully digital.

I’m currently working on a project for ComForCare San Fernando Valley, an in-home care agency that provides superior and personalized in-home health care services. I’m leading the redesign of the current caregiver app with a goal to reduce time spent on Payroll, prevent billing errors, and turn a manual and paper-heavy process fully digital.

​
​

Introducing e-commerce functionality to decrease Choisi’s bounce rate by 10%.

Choisi is an online fashion marketplace connecting fashion-conscious consumers to emerging independent designers from all over the world. In 2.5 weeks, my team and I delivered a full site redesign centered on seamless e-commerce functionality, including several ways the site can act as a personal stylist.

​
​

Designing a tool for users to create their own audio-guided walking tours

For this spec project, my team and I sought to see how Detour can enter the user-generated space, and see how users could create their own audio-guided tours with Detour. We designed an MVP of a creator platform that’s user-friendly, has a low barrier to entry and upholds Detour’s guidelines and standards. This creator tool can fit seamlessly into Detour’s existing app and users of all skill levels would receive help every step of the way.
